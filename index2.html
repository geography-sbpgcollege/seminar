Seminar Overview

Organized by: Dept of Geography, Shri Baldeo P. G. College.

This multidisciplinary national seminar explores the spatial dimensions of Human Resource Development (HRD). We are gathering scholars, policymakers, and geographers to discuss location-specific strategies in education, health, and skill development to contribute to the vision of a "Viksit Bharat @2047".
