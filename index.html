About the Seminar

Organized by: Department of Geography, Shri Baldeo P. G. College.

This multidisciplinary national seminar aims to explore the spatial dimensions of Human Resource Development (HRD). It brings together scholars, policymakers, and geographers to discuss how location-specific strategies in education, health, and skill development can contribute to the vision of a "Viksit Bharat @2047".
