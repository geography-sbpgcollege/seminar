Seminar Overview

Organized by: Department of Geography, Shri Baldeo P. G. College.

This multidisciplinary national seminar explores the spatial dimensions of Human Resource Development (HRD). We are gathering scholars, policymakers, and geographers to discuss location-specific strategies in education, health, and skill development to contribute to the vision of a "Viksit Bharat @2047".

Tentative Schedule

Day 1: Feb 21
Inauguration, Keynote Address & Technical Sessions I & II
Day 2: Feb 22
Technical Sessions III & IV, Valedictory Function & Certificate Distribution

Seminar Sub-Themes

  • 1. Spatial Dimensions of Education and Skill Development
  • 2. Geographies of Health, Wellness, and Nutrition
  • 3. Migration, Mobility, and Human Capital
  • 4. Gender, Social Inclusion, and Regional Development
  • 5. Environmental Sustainability, Climate Change, and Human Well-being
  • 6. Urbanisation, Smart Cities, and Quality of Life
  • 7. Rural Development, Agrarian Change, and Livelihood Security
  • 8. Geospatial Technologies in Human Resource Planning
  • 9. Geopolitics, Borders, and Human Security
  • 10. Policy, Governance, and Geographical Insights
  • 11. Any other sub-theme relevant to the theme
